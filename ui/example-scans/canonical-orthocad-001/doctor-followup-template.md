# Doctor Follow-Up Template

Use this as a patient-facing checklist before proceeding with aligner treatment.
Keep answers non-identifying if they are stored with sample or research data.

## Plan Intent

- What are the main clinical goals for this setup?
- Which teeth are expected to move the most, and in what direction?
- Are any teeth intentionally held still or excluded from movement?
- Is the displayed final bite a target setup, an approximation, or still pending revision?

## Attachments And IPR

- Which attachments are planned, on which teeth, and at which stages?
- Which IPR contacts are planned, how many millimeters at each contact, and when?
- Are any IPR amounts optional, staged, or dependent on tracking?
- Are there alternatives if IPR is not desired or cannot be completed as planned?

## Risk And Records

- Were radiographs or CBCT reviewed for root position, bone support, pathology, or restorations?
- Are there periodontal, recession, mobility, or root-resorption concerns?
- Are any movements considered higher risk or less predictable for this patient?
- What data would change the plan before trays are ordered?

## Tracking And Refinement

- How often should tracking be checked, and what signs mean trays are not tracking?
- Which teeth are most likely to need refinement?
- What is the expected refinement plan if alignment, bite, or contacts do not match the simulation?
- Can progress scans or photos be saved at key checkpoints for comparison?
