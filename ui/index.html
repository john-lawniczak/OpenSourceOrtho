      Getting Started

      1. Upload or reference a surface STL and confirm units before using millimeter checks.
      2. Declare which records are available; missing data limits what the engine can assess.
      3. Enter staged tooth movement, then review findings, acquisition advice, and report/export status.
      4. For physical workflows, use validated materials, confirmed scale, and calibrated equipment; aligner sheets are normally thermoformed over models, not simply printed and worn.
      5. Use dental model resin and selected thermoforming sheets such as PETG, TPU/polyurethane, or EVA-family materials; clean, cure, remove supports, and smooth printed models per material instructions.
